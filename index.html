Do you really wanna know me?

Believe me, it's nothing interesting..but if you want..

The first word I wrote to a computer using the command line was "Ciao(=hello)", but unfortunately he replied with "Command not found" but i saw that my older brother was able to do some interesting things on the pc, so to imitate him, I started looking for any resources available to learn how to communicate with the machine.

I really began to appreciate the beauty of a computer starting to play at Microsoft Encarta and watching my big brother play around with Windows XP.

I grew up with the idea of ​​wanting to know more and more everything about a computer, its applications and its limits. This led me to start a Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Engineering at University of Salerno in October 2015. In the same time i wanted to be free from my parents so firstly i started doing some casual job, to earn money and became independent, and after in 2019 i got a job in a software house as developer.
