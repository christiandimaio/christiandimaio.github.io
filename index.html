Do you really wanna know me?

Believe me, it's nothing interesting..but if you want..

The first word I wrote to a computer using the command line was "Ciao(=hello)", but unfortunately he replied with "Command not found" At the same time i saw that my older brother was able to do some interesting things on the pc, so to imitate him, I started looking for any resources available to learn how to communicate with the machine.

I really began to appreciate the beauty of a computer starting to play at Microsoft Encarta and watching my big brother play around with Windows XP.

I grew up with the idea of ​​wanting to know more and more about a computer, its applications and its limits. This led me to start a Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Engineering at University of Salerno in October 2015. In the same time i wanted to be free from my parents so firstly i started doing some casual job, to earn money and became independent, and after in 2019 i got a job in a software house as developer. I conclude my bachelor's in 2020 with an Internship at the National Institute of Geophysic and Volcanology of Naples and consequently a thesis on the realization of a web based application for managing the seismic network of the Osservatorio Vesuviano.

In October 2020 i moved from my hometown and started a Master course: Artificial Intelligence and Automation Engineering at University of Siena, because after taking notions on how machine works i want to learn how teach them.
